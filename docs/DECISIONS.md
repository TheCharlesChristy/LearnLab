| D-011 | §5.8, FR-PWA-002/003 | **Bug:** `python-bundle.zip` matched neither the PWA precache glob (`**/*.{js,css,html,svg,png,ico,woff2}` — no `.zip`) nor either `runtimeCaching` rule (it's same-origin but root-level, not under `/content/`, and not the Pyodide CDN prefix).
